PUPSRAAI Objectives


To assist the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Santa Rosa Campus to continue its role in clearing the path while laying new foundations to transform the institution into an epistemic community.

To provide an organized alumni assistance to Polytechnic University of the Philippines Santa Rosa Campus graduates.

To serve as a significant instrument thru which the alumni can reciprocately channel their resources in the promotion of higher education in the country.
